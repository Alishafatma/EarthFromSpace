Explore the protective shield surrounding our planet

Troposphere

The lowest layer where we live. Weather, clouds, and airplanes exist here. It contains most of the air we breathe.

Stratosphere

Contains the ozone layer which protects Earth from harmful UV rays. Commercial jets fly in this layer.

Mesosphere

Meteors burn up in this layer. It is the coldest layer of the atmosphere.

Thermosphere

Very high temperatures occur here. The International Space Station orbits in this layer.

Exosphere

The outermost layer. It gradually fades into outer space. Satellites orbit Earth here.
